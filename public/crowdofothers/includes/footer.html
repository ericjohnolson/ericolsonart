We Are a Crowd of Others is a series of performances, readings, and projects set within a site specific installation at MadArt Studio. The public is invited to visit during the Open Studio and observe the process as it unfolds.

This project is made possible by the generous support of MadArt with funding from Seattle Office of Arts and Culture and Artist Trust.
