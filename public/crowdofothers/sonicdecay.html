"Sonic Decay - A DIY Venue History" (Saturday, November 19th, 12PM - 6PM), Emory Liu with Special Guests

Sonic Decay will be a series of audio/visual based experiences focused on DIY music venues. Using audio and visuals from various sources the event will employ new media and analog technologies to remix, and re-enact the cyclical growth patterns of Seattle’s DIY music scene.
