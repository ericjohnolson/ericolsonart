RECESS GALLERY IS LOOKING FOR SUMMER INTERNS

RECESS Summer Internship Program 2015.RECESS is an independent arts center located in the SE industrial district of Portland dedicated to nurturing emergent, experimental, contemporary practices. RECESS aims to foster new forms of cultural inquiry and spark a discourse of change within both the art community and its wider context. For the past two years, RECESS has supported fresh ideas and perspectives from underrepresented artists, bringing them to light for the local arts community. As well as an exhibition space, RECESS also maintains an active community by renting out studios to local artists and hosting cutting edge music events. We’re now accepting applications for our five summer internship positions in Research, Development, Design, Exhibition, and Documentation. All interns will be given access to RECESS’ communal resources; wi-fi, kitchen, tools, and a shared office space. To apply, please send a cover letter, resume, and writing sample (and portfolio if applying for design position) to recesspdx@gmail.com and follow us online at recessart.com.
