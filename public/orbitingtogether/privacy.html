Orbiting Together’s Privacy Policy

Orbiting Together (OT) is an art project that uses Instagram to share responses to a text message notification system that syncs users to satellites flying over the Seattle Art Museum.

This Privacy Policy describes how and when OT collects, uses and shares information when you use our service. OT receives your information through our APIs, applications and from our partners and other third parties. For example, you send us information when you use our OT app. When using any of our Services you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Irrespective of which country you reside in or supply information from, you authorize OT to use your information in the United States and any other country where OT operates.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@orbitingtogether.com.

Information Collection and Use

We only collect responses to the Orbiting Together notifications and post them back on our own profile, we don't collect any information from users submitting photographic or video responses to the project.

Share, Re-post & Settings: OT is designed to help you share your responses to the project, and show the different embodiment exerices we are performing together. We will not share any of the users information expect when tagging you in a repost.

Location Information: We do not use location information.

Links: We do not track any of your usage data.

Cookies: We do not collect information from cookies of where you have browsed.

Log Data: We do not log any data.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not disclose your private personal information except in the limited circumstances described here.

Your Consent: We may share or disclose your information only when you give us consent to the Orbiting Together instagram account, and those responses are shared on the website.

Law and Harm: Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this Policy, we may preserve or disclose your information if we believe that it is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request; to protect the safety of any person; to address fraud, security or technical issues; or to protect Grab for Instagram's rights or property. However, nothing in this Privacy Policy is intended to limit any legal defenses or objections that you may have to a third party, including a government, request to disclose your information.

Business Transfers and Affiliates: In the event that OT is involved in a bankruptcy, merger, acquisition, reorganization or sale of assets, your information may be sold or transferred as part of that transaction. This Privacy Policy will apply to your information as transferred to the new entity.

Non-Private or Non-Personal Information: We will not share or disclose your non-private, aggregated or otherwise non-personal information.
