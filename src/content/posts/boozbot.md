Recently I finished working on a version 1 of the communication and brain framework for a robotic bartender [Jeff Crouse](http://www.jeffcrouse.info) and [David Jimison](http://www.davidjimison.info) dreamt up and coined BoozBot.  The idea was to create a robot that is reminiscent of the forgotten service robots depicted in movies of our past.  The end result was a very interesting interactive bartender that captured interesting insight to how people expect robots to behave and interact in our society.  Hope you enjoy!
