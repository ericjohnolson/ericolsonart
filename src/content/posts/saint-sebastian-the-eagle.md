My newest screen print was created for my sister, and follows some of the current ideas bouncing around in my head related to hope, belief, life, death, and the frailty/impermanence surronding them.  The screens were exposed at an amazing studio in LIC called [Post Expose](http://www.postexpose.com).  Anyways this one is for the family. Respect queens and saints.  Alas [Saint Sebastian](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Sebastian) the Eagle.
