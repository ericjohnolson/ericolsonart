The following is the artwork for the latest [Man Plus](http://www.manplusrocks.com) album due the end of summer.  I will post some updates when we finish the full layout.  The album will be named A History of Ugliness and was partly inspired by an amazing art history book called [On Ugliness](http://www.amazon.com/Ugliness-Umberto-Eco/dp/0847829863) by Umberto Eco.  Jared and I had a series of ideas bouncing from coast to coast until we came up with the idea of having a series of collaged monster heads categorized on the forgotten shelves of an old biologist's laboratory.  I hope you enjoy, also I have heard a few of the rough cuts and the new music sounds as magical as ever.
