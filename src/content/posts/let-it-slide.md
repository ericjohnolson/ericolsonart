Timothy Firth and I are embarking on a personal challenge of actualizing a series of one day projects. These projects act as prototypes, visual and participatory tests, that can take us no more than a day to create and install.

We have done a couple of these in the past, but the latest one is a slide that I used in "Sold at a loss" reimagined as a water slide. The instructions state:

	1. Think of something weighing heavy on your heart.
        2. Let it slide.
