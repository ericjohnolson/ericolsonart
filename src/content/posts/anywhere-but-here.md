I started collaborating with an [Eyebeam](http://www.eyebeam.org) fellow [Jeff Crouse](http://www.jeffcrouse.info) and resident [David Jimison](http://www.davidjimison.info) to create interactive party technology.  My first contribution was for a project called [Anywhere But Here](http://www.digitalsituations.com/awbh/), which allowed people at a party to pretend to be at a cooler party where they were being photographed.  The project was inspired by the fact that photographers, like the ones from [Last Night's Party](http://www.lastnightsparty.com), have become the new DJ of a party.  Therefore playing into this, people selected a party to pretend to be at while photographers took their photos and then they uploaded them to their social networking sites using a software kiosk I created called the photo blaster.


 


For more information about the project check out the [Digital Situations](http://www.digitalsituations.com/awbh/) website, it provides more hilarious photos and a more complete description of the project.
