## Installation at ACT Theatre



Timothy Firth, Foster Turcott and my installation, A Comedy in Four Acts, comes down this month, so if you haven't been able to check it out yet, go soon!  It is a 70ft+ installation of florescent light bulbs and 2-way mirrored boxes exploring desire.  A VERY SPECIAL thanks to everyone who helped support the project through our Cheap Tricks Fun(d)raiser!
