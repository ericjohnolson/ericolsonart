After much reflection, and soul searching, Timothy and I have decided to take on the huge feat of exploring Timothy's psychological condition, in which his wild wild west predispositions, emotions, and thoughts are seperated from the rest of his psyche.

The work will be a social experiment openly and publicly displaying a very private aspect of a person's life, their subconscious defense mechanisms which hide their inner most fears and vulnerabilities.
