“Let it Slide” is one of a series of one day projects Timothy Firth and I have created. These projects act as prototypes, visual and participatory tests, that can take us no more than a day to create and install.

Using a slide from the “Sold at a Loss” installation, we created a water slide with the following instructions:

1. Think of something weighing heavy on your heart.

2. Let it slide.
