Automatic Messaging Medium is an installation consisting of a toll-free phone message service, wheat-pasted advertisements, and a projected hologram of collected pop culture representations of ghosts. Participants are invited to leave a message for one of their ghosts on the toll-free hotline. Audio of recorded messages plays alongside the video holograms. This project was supported by Spaceworks Tacoma, and was installed at the Tollbooth Gallery in Tacoma, WA from December 2016 – March 2017.
