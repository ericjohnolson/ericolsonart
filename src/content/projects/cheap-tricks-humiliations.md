To raise money for the “A Comedy in Four Acts” installation at ACT Theatre, Timothy Firth, Foster Turcott and I resorted to dares for any donation.  Many questions were asked and explored.  How will you exploit your friends?  How low will you go for a quick buck? Will people donate what something is worth, or will the take advantage of the donation based system?  What feats will a hunger artist do to get their work finished?
