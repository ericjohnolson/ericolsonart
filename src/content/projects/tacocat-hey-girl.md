James Smith and I created a stop-animation music video for Tacocat’s album *NVM*, out now on Hardly Art.

Press via [Stereogum](http://www.stereogum.com/1673289/tacocat-hey-girl-video-stereogum-premiere/video/): “and with directors Eric J. Olson and James Smith they now have this very funny, striking video. Cut-out figures that resemble animated paper dolls perfectly visualize “Hey Girl,” their funny, sarcastic attack on that repulsive creature: the catcalling asshole. The characters described in the song appear here as gaping troglodytes, while the main character skates by leaving them all in the dust.”
