For this project I hand printed as many posters as I could until I had to quit from exhaustion. The prints promoted [Le Petit Nickel](http://ericolson.xyz/index.php/projects/le-petit-nickel/) and were posted up on telephone polls in neighborhoods around Seattle soliciting labor for “meaningful” unpaid opportunities in the arts.
