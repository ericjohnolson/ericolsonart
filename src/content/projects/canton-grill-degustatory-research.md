Portland’s oldest Chinese-American restaurant, [Canton Grill](http://www.canton-grill.com/), has prodigiously thrived through the generations with continued resilience and adaptation. Today, Chinese-American food is a hybrid of its own, prepared by chefs from China with their own narratives of adaptation. Investigating both personal and collective histories alongside the role of food in migration, *Canton Grill Degustatory Research* is a sensorial ten-course tasting experience serving samples intermingling culinary art, performance, research and personal histories. The event promised to be a beautiful, complex, fine dining experience that tickled palettes and teased one's soul.
