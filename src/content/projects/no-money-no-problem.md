Originally envisioned as a series of free relational/communication therapy sessions that are bugged, mistranslated, and remixed into videos. For this project I programmed a lolcat translator, text to speech software and Google hangouts to obfuscate the identities of the participants. This project has yet to be fully realized as a series.
